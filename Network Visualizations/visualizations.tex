\begin{figure}[!htb]

		\includegraphics[width=1\textwidth]{{citations1}.pdf}
		\caption{Supreme Court Citation Network, 1937--2002. Nodes are supreme court cases, with size based on incoming citations. Salient  cases (\textit{Oxford Guide}) are in yellow. Each network layer contains cases decided under a different chief justice. Because our data is temporally bound, cases for the Hughes and Rehnquist courts are not complete. Edges between two nodes indicate the later document cites the earlier one.}
	\end{center}
\end{figure}

The transitive structure formed by the three named cases is highlighted in pink.}

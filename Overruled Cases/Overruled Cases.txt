This data set contains information on US Supreme Court decisions overruled by subsequent decision. 
The raw data is obtained from US Government Publishing Office publication:
"Supreme Court Decisions Overruled by Subsequent Decision" pp. 2601-2615 in Constitution of the United States of America: Analysis and Interpretation (Centennial Ed.). (2016)
The document is available from: https://www.gpo.gov/fdsys/granule/GPO-CONAN-REV-2016/GPO-CONAN-REV-2016-13

This data set contains three columns:
1) Overruling case (US Reporter ID);
2) Overruled case (US Reporter ID);
3) Whether overruling was not explicitly stated (dummy variable).

Two notes:
1) 465 U.S. 89 (no. 177), Pennhurst State School & Hosp. v. Halderman (1984) has 28 additional cases that ``more or less fall under the Pennhurst Doctrine''. (See p. 2612) 
-We included only the initial five listed.
2) Four cases from 570 (no. 233-236) onward (forming six edges) do not have US Reporter IDs. (See p. 2615)
-These are not included in our data.
